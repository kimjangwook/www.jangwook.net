## 6. Key Insights: 5 Truths from the Data

### 6.1 Organic Search is the Growth Engine

<strong>Data</strong>: 56.5% organic search (1,970 sessions from Google)

<strong>Analysis</strong>:
- Up 12%p from previous 45 days (44.3%)

<strong>Recommendation</strong>: Expand content around popular keywords (Gemini RAG, MCP, Claude)

### 6.2 China is the New Growth Driver

<strong>Data</strong>: China 690 users (26% of total), #2 country

<strong>Analysis</strong>:
- Rose to #2 market one month after adding Chinese support (Nov 17)
- Claude Skills Guide, Gemini tutorial are popular

<strong>Recommendation</strong>: Strengthen content focused on Chinese developer interests (AI tools, automation)

### 6.3 AI Platforms are New Referral Channels

<strong>Data</strong>: ChatGPT(88) + Perplexity(32) + Gemini(16) = 136 sessions

<strong>Analysis</strong>:
- 5x traditional social media (27 sessions)

### 6.4 Content Quality Proven by Duration

<strong>Data</strong>: Chinese Gemini tutorial average duration 21:06

<strong>Analysis</strong>:
- Top landing pages average 4-5 minute duration
- Readers genuinely engaging with in-depth tutorial content

<strong>Data</strong>: Homepage bounce rate 78.8%, duration 1:08

<strong>Analysis</strong>:
- Search-acquired users exit without exploring after landing
- Lack of guidance from homepage to interesting content

<strong>Recommendation</strong>: Add popular content section and recommended post cards to homepage
